Tic Tac Toe Mini Game

This is a game for two players who take turns marking the spaces in a three-by-three grid with X or O. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row is the winner.

With this game structure, I start thinking the game function:

  • There are two players in this game.
  • Player will take turn when clicked on one of the boxes.
  • Check winner (three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row is the winner.)
  • Check draw.
  • Let player restart the game when one of them win or game draw.

Then, I create the 9x9 ui design, make the data object and event handler function.

Rotated animation on banner

This page is a product display page, company have some requests on the layout design:

  • They want three images to be flips.
  • They want to show the campaign logo.
  • They want to mention three offers, in the meanwhile highlight the always on offer.
  • They want to have two option of filters.

Based on the company style and nature, I make a fade-in-out infinite animation on the banner. Separated three offers into three parts which highlighted the always on offer and can be very easy to maintain if any of them needed to be remove when offer expired. For the filters, I only display either one of them when page loaded so as to save space.
